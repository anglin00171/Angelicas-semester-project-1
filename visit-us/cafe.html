SAGA Science Museum: Café

Café

Food and drinks

Discover a Taste of Artistry at our Café that are attached to the museum when you are here!Here you can get light lunches, soft drinks, coffee, snacks and more.

Embark on a culinary journey where every bite and sip is a masterpiece, nestled in the heart of our Museum café. Our café isn't just a place to eat; it's a destination where the passion for art and the love for exquisite food and drinks converge. We have a menu that is a vibrant canvas, featuring locally sourced ingredients and seasonal specialties. From artisanal sandwiches and fresh salads to delectable pastries and desserts.

Pair your meal with our selection of fine wines, craft beers, or one of our signature coffee blends for a perfect accompaniment.
