Privacy Promise

Privacy Promise for SAGA Science Museum

At SAGA Science Museum, your privacy and the privacy of your children are of utmost importance to us. We are committed to transparently managing, securing, and keeping your personal data safe.

Personal Information:

We only collect personal information that is necessary for our services, such as, registering for events, or sending newsletters. This information typically includes names, contact details, and sometimes age, especially when related to children's educational programs.

Purpose and Consent:

Before collecting any personal data, we explain the purposes for which it will be used and seek explicit consent from you or a guardian, especially when it concerns children under the age of 15.

Children’s Data:

Special attention is given to information collected from children. We ensure that it is appropriate to their age and that we obtain consent from a parent or guardian where necessary.

Data Sharing:

You have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal information we hold about you or your children. We maintain a high level of transparency about our data practices and are always available to answer your questions and address any concerns regarding your privacy.

Updates to Our Privacy Promise

We regularly review and update our privacy practices and this promise to ensure ongoing compliance with the latest regulations and global best practices. Any updates to our Privacy Promise will be communicated through our website and, where applicable, through direct communication channels.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our privacy practices or wish to exercise any of your rights, please contact us at:
