Saturday: 9:00 – 16:00

Sunday: 9:00 – 13:00

Admission and Guided Tours

The entrance is free for all. There are guided tours of the museum that leave every hour. These tours are 70 NOK per person and include a handy printed guide of the museum.

If you would like to organise a guided tour for your group of 6 or more people, please contact us to arrange the tour.

Accessibility

The museum has wheelchair accessibility ramps. It also has audio guides and braille display signs for the visually impaired.

If there is any questions please contact us

Location

The museum is located at Waldemar Thranes gate in Oslo. There is a river called Akerselva that floats nearby the museum as well.

How to Get Here

If you are driving into Oslo from Major Highways:

Oslo is encircled by the E6 motorway from the north and south, and the E18 from the east and west. If you're coming from any direction, you'll find yourself on one of these roads.

Entering Oslo:

Follow the signs towards Oslo’s city center. The E6 and E18 have clear signage.

Towards Waldemar Thranes gate:

As you approach the city center, look for signs pointing towards “Ring 2”, a major ring road that circles the inner part of Oslo.

On Ring 2:

Waldemar Thranes gate is located directly off of “Ring 2”. You will find it between the neighborhoods of St. Hanshaugen and Grünerløkka.

If you are arriving by train

Arrival Station:

Most likely, you’ll arrive at Oslo Central Station (Oslo S), the main railway station.

To Waldemar Thranes gate:

From Oslo S, you can take a tram or bus. The tram lines 11, 12, and 13, as well as several bus lines, stop near Waldemar Thranes gate. The tram stop you might use is called “Alexander Kiellands Plass” which is close by.

If you are flying

Main Airport:

Oslo Gardermoen (OSL) is the main international airport.

Airport to City Center:

You can take the Flytoget (Airport Express Train) to Oslo Central Station (Oslo S) which takes about 20 minutes. Alternatively, there are buses.

From Oslo S to Waldemar Thranes gate:

Follow the train directions above to reach Waldemar Thranes gate from Oslo Central Station.

If arriving by long-distance bus:

Bus Terminal:

You’ll arrive at the Oslo Bus Terminal, adjacent to Oslo Central Station.

To Waldemar Thranes gate:

Follow the directions provided in the train section above.

There is Public Transport Apps:

Recommend using real-time public transport apps like Ruter’s official app for Oslo and Akershus, which provides detailed routes and schedules.

Google Maps:

For those unfamiliar with the city, the use of Google Maps can be extremely helpful. It offers directions for driving, public transport, walking, and biking
