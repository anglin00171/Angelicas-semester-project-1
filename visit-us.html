Opening Hours

The entrance is free for all. Our opening hours are:

Monday: Closed

Tuesday: 10:00 – 16:00

Wednesday: 10:00 – 16:00

Thursday: 10:00 – 16:00

Friday: 10:00 – 19:00

Saturday: 9:00 – 16:00

Sunday: 9:00 – 13:00

Admission and Guided Tours

The entrance is free for all. There are guided tours of the museum that leave every hour. These tours are 70 NOK per person and include a handy printed guide of the museum.

If you would like to organise a guided tour for your group of 6 or more people, please contact us to arrange the tour.
